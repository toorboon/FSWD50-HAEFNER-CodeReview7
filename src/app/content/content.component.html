Welcome to CodeReview 7

This CodeReview is about a Phone Book App. That is the reason why this app was developed for mobile devices up to 640pixel in width. You can check your important numbers and also add new ones, as well as editing them and deleting them. In addition you have a filter available for searching through your numbers.

Used Technology
  • Angular 6
  • TypeScript
  • Bootstrap 4
Feature List:
  • Manage your important numbers!
  • Manage saved numbers!
  • Add new records!
  • Filter your record set!
